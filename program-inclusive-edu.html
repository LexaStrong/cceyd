Inclusive Education

Ensuring quality education and school partnerships for all children.

Description & Objectives

Our Inclusive Education program is designed to create lasting, systemic change at the grassroots level. By focusing on education, direct intervention, and advocacy, we empower communities to take charge of their future.

Current Projects & Initiatives

  • School Accessibility Upgrades
  • Teacher Training on Special Needs
  • Scholarship Programs

Impact & Beneficiaries

Provided scholarships for 200+ marginalized youth and trained 50 teachers.
